Welcome to Hoppy Days!

The newest beer garden in Harrisburg is all the buzz! 20 million 5-star reviews can't be wrong.

Sunny, grassy, lots of over-sized games, friends, and of course, BEER!

Hoppy Days beer garden is the perfect place to relax with friends and family or host a birthday or corporate event!
